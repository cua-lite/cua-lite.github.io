Why CUA-Lite — and a call for contributors.

CUA-Lite exists to democratize computer-use agents: everything an agent needs — data, benchmarks, training — made open to share and simple to run. The platform is the where; the framework is the how. Both grow by contribution, and here is where a pull request lands hardest:

Datasets

Convert a dataset to LiteSample and everyone training a computer-use agent can train on it — every agent, every training stack, one pull request. For dataset authors, that means users and citations far beyond a standalone release. And for everyone who wants to train but has no data: it's already here, free on Hugging Face — 10+ datasets plus a growing set of GPT-5.5 rollouts.

Benchmarks

Every env here speaks the same action space, so a benchmark integrated once runs every agent — today's and tomorrow's. And even without integrating anything, an eval you already ran is worth submitting: scores land on the leaderboard as committed, reproducible JSON.
